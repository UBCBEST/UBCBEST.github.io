We Are UBC BEST

University of British Columbia's
Biomedical Engineering Student Team

 About

We are a multi-disciplinary team of students from the Faculty of Applied Science (Engineering), Science, Sauder School of Business, Kinesiology, Arts and Medicine focused on developing context appropriate solutions to medical challenges worldwide.

 Vision

Build a multidisciplinary community that cultivates networking, technical growth, and collaboration in the biomedical field at UBC.

 Mission

  • Successfully implement relevant technology in the medical community to optimize care.
  • Develop our members both technically and professionally.
  • Promote student learning of entrepreneurship in the biomedical field.

 Undergrad Student

UBC BEST consistently recruits talented undergraduate students to contribute to our projects. Please refer to our active postings to see if we are currently recruiting for specific positions.
If you feel as if you are not technically experienced for the open roles, be sure to check out our Mentorship Program.

 Graduate Student

Although UBC BEST does not have any graduate students working as technical members, we are always interested in speaking to potential advisors. Currently, we have multiple graduate students who contribute to our team by acting as an advisor to one or more of the projects. If you think you had skills that could benefit BEST, please get in touch!

 Medical Professional

UBC BEST is always looking for new projects to work on that address challenges in the medical field. If you have an idea, would like to get involved as an advisor, or just want to hear more about our team then we would appreciate the opportunity to meet with you.

 Industry Member

UBC BEST benefits greatly from advisors in the engineering field. If you are interested in learning more about BEST and contributing to our team, then we would appreciate the opportunity to get in touch and explore areas for collaboration.
